Why this matters

AI changes security work on both sides.

Defender gains

  • Turn noisy alerts into readable summaries.
  • Compare notes, indicators, and timelines faster.
  • Draft cleaner reports, queries, and handoffs.
  • Reduce repetitive cognitive load in daily operations.

Attacker gains

  • Faster phishing and social engineering content.
  • Quicker OSINT and recon synthesis.
  • Scaled low-effort script and content generation.
  • Improved speed even when skill level stays average.
